Service is a god


Doctors have a thorough understanding of diseases, their symptoms, and treatments. They use their knowledge and medical devices to diagnose and treat patients.

Doctors work hard and selflessly to help others and improve the health of society. They are available whenever needed, even in emergencies.

Doctors are responsible for the health of society and take on many duties to ensure that patients receive the care they need.

Doctors provide support, guidance, and comfort to patients and their families. They try to cheer up patients and make them feel comfortable.

Doctors play a very essential role in human life. We can say Doctors are the incarnation of the god. In India, we celebrate National Doctor's Day on July of 1st to thank physicians and doctors for their dedicated services to patients.
